1 Drop Blood, 1 Life


Blood Management website initiated by NRCS Baliya for helping those in need.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a centralized platform that connects blood banks and donors, making it easier for patients to access life-saving blood transfusions.

Vision

We envision a world where no one suffers due to the unavailability of blood. Through our platform, we strive to ensure that every individual in need receives timely and adequate blood supplies.
